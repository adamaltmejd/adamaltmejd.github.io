I am a researcher at the Institute for Evaluation of Labor Market and Education Policy (IFAU) in Uppsala, an IZA research affiliate, and a Distinguished CESifo Affiliate. I received my Ph.D. from Stockholm School of Economics in 2018.

I do research on inequality, education, and health—in applied microeconomics—using extensive individual-level datasets to to study the causes and consequences of important real-life decisions. I am also coordinating SWECOV, a large research program about the COVID-19 pandemic.
